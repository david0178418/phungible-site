Phungible Privacy Policy

Introduction

Protecting customer privacy is Tanado LLCs (“Tanado”, “we”, “us”) most important task. To this end the following Privacy Policy explains how Tanado collects and uses personal information that is provided to us by you when you visit Phungible.com (the “Website”) or use the Phungible mobile application (the “App”). “Personal information” is defined as information that identifies you, such as your name, address, phone number, photo, or email address.

The Type of Information Tanado Collects and How We Collect It

We, via the Website and the App, may collect two primary types of data:

  • Personal information (as defined above) and
  • technical information and navigational information which includes but is not limited to the following:
    • web browser type
    • operating system
    • IP address
    • pages visited/time spent
    • error reports

This information is collected:

  • Directly from you when you provide it (i.e. Website forms, subscriptions, error reporting, etc. ) and/or
  • automatically (through the use of cookies or other information tracking technologies) as you use the Website or App.

How Tanado Uses Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise directly profit of off your information. We do use your information for the following:

  • To fulfill the purpose for which you provided the information.
  • To carry out any legal obligations we have.
  • To enforce our contractual rights.
  • To provide you with notice in relation to changes to the Website and/or App and any related agreements and policies.
  • To enhance our market and product research, troubleshoot problems, improve customer service/software performance, and to detect errors and criminal activity.

    • Updates to the Privacy Policy

    We update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date listed below is the last date it was revised. If any changes are made to the Policy those changes will take immediate effect when the new policy is posted on the Website. If you have any questions, concerns, or comments please contact us at contact@phungible.com.
